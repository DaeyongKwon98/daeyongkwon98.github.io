I am a Ph.D. student at the [**Language and Data Intelligence Lab at Seoul National University**](https://ldilab.github.io/), advised by [**Prof. Seungwon Hwang**](https://seungwonh.github.io/).
My research focuses on multi-hop reasoning in Large Language Models, evaluation and benchmark design for LLMs, retrieval-augmented generation, and multimodal learning. I am particularly interested in improving the reliability and interpretability of reasoning systems, with applications spanning knowledge-intensive QA and music information retrieval.
